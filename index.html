Biography

I will be joining EPFL as a PhD student in Electrical Engineering, working with Prof. Colin Jones. I received my MPhil from the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am fortunate to work with Prof. Minghua Chen during my MPhil research. Prior to that, I received my BEng in Electronic Engineering and (dual) BSc in Math, both from Tsinghua University in 2018. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Xing Li and Prof. Stephen Shing-Toung Yau during my undergraduate.

Research Interests

My current research interests include optimization, control, machine learning and their applications to operation and decision-making problems arising from real-world scenarios such as transportation, building and manufacturing.
